COME AND CELEBRATE WHAT YOU CREATE WITH TECHNOLOGY

Dev Fest is an event celebrating the creativity of amazing developers and designers around us. It provides developers a stage to share their knowledge and learn from others. Startups looking for the right people, Open source authors showcasing their latest creations and App developers displaying their labors of love all under one roof! And what's more? It's free!
